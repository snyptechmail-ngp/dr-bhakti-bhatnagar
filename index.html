Our Services

Comprehensive Eye Care Solutions

From routine eye exams to advanced surgical procedures, we offer a full spectrum of ophthalmological care for patients of all ages.

Cataract Surgery

State-of-the-art phacoemulsification and premium IOL implantation for crystal-clear vision restoration with minimal recovery time.

Pediatric Ophthalmology

Specialized eye care for children including comprehensive eye exams, amblyopia management, and early detection of vision problems.

Squint Correction

Expert strabismus evaluation and surgical correction for children and adults, restoring proper eye alignment and binocular vision.

Laser Eye Surgery

Advanced laser procedures for refractive error correction, diabetic retinopathy treatment, and glaucoma management.

Comprehensive Eye Exam

Thorough eye health evaluation using advanced diagnostic equipment for accurate prescriptions and early disease detection.

Glaucoma Management

Expert diagnosis and treatment of glaucoma with medical therapy, laser treatment, and surgical options to preserve vision.
